About Me

Growing up in Miami, you develop an everlasting taste for carribean foods. Having moved to
North Carolina at the age of 6, has left me forever longing for the foods that my Grandmother
raised and nurtured me with. Now, at 38 years of age, I've decided that there was no need for
me to have to go without. My agging desires to satisfy my pallette with savory flavors of stewed
tomatoes, sofrito, and the starchiness of guineo and potatoes.
